About Me

I believe great design is a dialogue between a space and its inhabitants. My design philosophy is centered on 'human-centric modernism'—creating environments that are not only beautiful and functional but also resonate with the personalities and lifestyles of those who live and work within them.

With a background in architecture from Sir J.J. College of Architecture and a passion for sustainable materials, I bring a holistic approach to every project. From our studio in Mumbai, we collaborate with clients to translate their vision into timeless, thoughtful, and inspiring interiors.

Selected Works

A collection of projects that showcase my approach to design and problem-solving.

Serene Juhu Apartment

Location: Mumbai, Maharashtra | Type: Residential | Year: 2025

The client's vision was a peaceful retreat from the city's dynamism. We employed a neutral palette, abundant natural light, and bespoke oak millwork to create a calming, clutter-free sanctuary. The design emphasizes texture and natural materials, fostering a connection to the serene coastal surroundings.

The Artisan's Corner Café

Location: Bandra, Mumbai | Type: Commercial/Hospitality | Year: 2024

This project was about crafting a community hub. We blended industrial elements with warm, artisanal touches, like handmade tiles and locally sourced wood. The layout encourages interaction, with a mix of cozy nooks and communal tables, creating an inviting atmosphere that feels like a home away from home.

Alibaug Holiday Home

Location: Alibaug, Maharashtra | Type: Residential | Year: 2023

Designed as a weekend getaway, this villa blurs the lines between indoors and outdoors. Large glass panels, a central courtyard, and natural materials like laterite stone and teak wood create a seamless flow. The interiors are relaxed yet sophisticated, perfect for unwinding and entertaining.
